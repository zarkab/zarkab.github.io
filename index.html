    Power Electronics based Solutions for Electric Vehicle Charging Infrastructure

    Various issues pertaining to On-board EV chargers are currently being investigated. The issues which are under investigation are DC-link capacitor size, power loss reduction using ZVS, non-linear inductors and IOBEV’s. For hardware implementation 1kW SiC based VSC has been developed and the microcontroller employed is Texas Instruments’ TMS320f28379D.

    Jan 2021 - Present

    Cooperative Isolated Renewable Energy Systems for Enhancing Reliability

    An easy-to-implement, reliable and voltage sensorless active power decoupling control based on model predictive control (MPC) scheme for buck-based APD circuit was implemented. By extrapolating the existing MPC control for APD and modifying it to a voltage-vector oriented control, a sub-optimal solution to track the reference voltage vector without sensing the decoupling voltage was presented. Due to voltage-vector oriented based modulated model predictive control (MMPC) scheme and decoupling voltage sensor elimination, the execution time of the processor is reduced, which provides a provision to approach high switching frequency. To validate the decoupling performance of the voltage sensorless MMPC control, simulation and experimental studies show that the grid power quality is maintained with reduced DC-link ripple in both ideal and non-ideal grid conditions. The MMPC control was implemented with 300W IGBT based 3-Leg VSI using Texas Instruments microcontroller (TMS320f28379D).

    Jan 2020 - March 2022

    Comparsion of Different Modulation Strategies for 3-Phase VSI

    In this work, various modulation techniques of a three-phase voltage source converter were compared, analyzed and examined for their performance in generation for a sinusoidal output. Modulation techniques include Sine-PWM, Space-vector PWM, Delta-Sigma, Model Predictive Control and Modulated Model Predictive Control. MATLAB/Simulink and Hardware implementation of different modulation techniques was verified on 1kW IGBT based 3-Phase VSI using D-Space(RTI 1104).
